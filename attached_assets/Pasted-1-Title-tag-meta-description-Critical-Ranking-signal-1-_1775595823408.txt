2. Custom domain + SSL
Critical
Domain authority blocker
Problem

3. Structured data (Schema.org)
Critical
Rich snippets in search
Add JSON-LD to every page
Types needed: LocalBusiness, Product, Review, BreadcrumbList. Include: business name, address, phone, geo coords, openingHours, priceRange, image, sameAs (social links). This unlocks star ratings, business panels, and product carousels in Google.
