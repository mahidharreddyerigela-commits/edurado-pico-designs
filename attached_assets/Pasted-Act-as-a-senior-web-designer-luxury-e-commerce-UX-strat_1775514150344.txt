Act as a senior web designer, luxury e-commerce UX strategist, and front-end developer. Build a high-end, modern, conversion-focused website for a brand called “Eduardo Pico Designs.”

Brand Overview:
Eduardo Pico Designs sells custom crafted decor and personalized products, including:
- Door Hangers
- Tumblers
- Trays
- Boards
- Business Branded products
- Custom Awards

The brand should feel:
- Elegant
- Creative
- Warm
- Handmade but premium
- Trustworthy for both personal and business customers

Main Goal:
Create a visually stunning, polished e-commerce website that feels premium and boutique-level, while still being easy to shop. The site should encourage product browsing, trust, and inquiries for custom work.

Design Direction:
- Clean, luxury-modern layout
- Soft neutral background tones with tasteful accent colors
- Minimal but artistic styling
- Premium typography with a mix of elegant serif + clean sans-serif
- Smooth animations and subtle hover effects
- High-end product showcase feel
- Spacious layout with strong visual hierarchy
- Mobile-first and fully responsive

Website Structure:
1. Hero Section
- Large premium hero banner
- Brand name: Eduardo Pico Designs
- Tagline options like:
  “Crafted Decor. Personalized Impact.”
  or
  “Custom Pieces Designed to Stand Out.”
- CTA buttons:
  - Shop All
  - Explore Collections
  - Request Custom Work

2. Intro / Welcome Section
- Include messaging inspired by:
  “Welcome to our store”
  “Grow your business with us”
  “Outstanding service | Exclusive pricing | Industry expertise”
- Refine this into polished professional copy
- Make the section feel warm, inviting, and premium

3. Featured Collections Section
Create beautiful collection cards for:
- Business Branded
- Door Hangers
- Custom Awards
- Tumblers
- Trays & Boards
- Shop All

Each collection card should have:
- Elegant product imagery placeholders
- Hover animation
- Shop now button
- Short premium description

4. About the Brand Section
Tell the story of Eduardo Pico Designs as a creative custom decor brand that helps customers celebrate, brand, gift, and decorate with meaningful custom-made pieces.
Make the writing feel authentic, boutique, and premium.

5. Business / Custom Work Section
Highlight that Eduardo Pico Designs also helps businesses with branded products and custom orders.
Include benefits such as:
- Custom branding
- Bulk ordering
- Unique handcrafted presentation
- Personalized service
- Quality materials and detail-focused craftsmanship

Add CTA:
- Start a Custom Order
- Contact Us

6. Nonprofit / Giving Back Section
Create a meaningful “Giving Back” section showing support for nonprofit organizations.

Feature these two nonprofits:
- Frontline Heroes Outdoors
  https://www.frontlineheroesoutdoors.org/
- Hometown Hero Outdoors
  https://hometownherooutdoors.org/

This section should communicate that the business supports causes that help heroes through healing outdoor experiences.
Add elegant cards with short descriptions and “Learn More” buttons linking to each organization.

7. Contact / Collaboration Section
Include a section for:
“If you wish to work with us, please contact us.”
Make it polished and inviting.
Include:
- contact form
- email placeholder
- social links
- CTA button: Let’s Work Together

8. Footer
Include refined footer navigation with:
- Search
- About Us
- Contact
- Terms of Service
- Refund Policy
- Privacy Policy
- Contact Information
- Shop All Products

Also include:
- social media icons
- payment method icons
- copyright:
  © 2026, Eduardo Pico Designs

Functional Requirements:
- Clean responsive navigation bar
- Cart icon
- Login/account option
- Announcement bar at top
- Featured products / best sellers section
- Testimonial or trust-building section
- Beautiful product grid layout
- Strong CTA sections throughout

Homepage Copy Tone:

- Include rounded cards or soft-edged sections
